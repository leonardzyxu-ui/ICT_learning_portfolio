Digital Media Mastery

Decoding the DNA of images.

Raster = "Rake"

I learned that "Raster" comes from the Latin rastrum (rake). This is the perfect analogy: an electron beam "rakes" across the screen row by row to paint pixels.

  • ✓ Best for: Photographs and complex blending.
  • ✓ Tool: GIMP (Pixel Manipulation).
  • ✕ Weakness: Pixelates when zoomed.

Vector = Math

Vectors are defined by Magnitude and Direction. They aren't pixels; they are mathematical instructions. I verified this by opening an SVG in a text editor:

<circle cx="50" cy="50" r="40" />
<!-- Code defines the shape! -->

The "Liquid Glass" Incident

I attempted to create a complex "Liquid Glass" distortion effect. I tried standard CSS filters. I tried WebGL. Nothing worked—it just slowed down the site.

"Instead of giving up, I wrote a professional email to my teacher (Jaydon). I explained the technical blocker, attached my failed WebGL attempts, and negotiated a design change."

This taught me that Communication > Brute Force.
